About us

Welcome to AutoHQ, Your Trusted Automotive Community

At AutoHQ, we live and breathe cars. Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate car enthusiasts, our forum has grown into one of the most respected online communities for automotive lovers around the world. What started as a small online gathering of friends sharing their love for vehicles has evolved into a vibrant hub where thousands of members come together to discuss everything on wheels.

Our Story

AutoHQ was born from a simple idea: to create a place where people from all walks of life could share their passion for automobiles, exchange knowledge, and help each other navigate the complexities of car ownership. Our founders—Jake Thompson, Maria Ramirez, and Alex Wu—were lifelong friends who bonded over a shared love of classic cars and long road trips.

Jake, a professional mechanic with over 20 years of experience, Maria, an automotive journalist with a knack for spotting industry trends, and Stefan and Todor, software engineers with a vision for building online communities, pooled their talents to create AutoHQ. Their goal was to establish a forum that wasn't just about cars, but also about the people who love them.

Our Mission

Our mission at AutoHQ is to connect car enthusiasts, industry experts, and everyday drivers in a welcoming, inclusive environment where they can share their experiences, ask questions, and gain insights. We believe that knowledge should be accessible to everyone, and our forum is designed to be a place where both beginners and experts can learn from each other.

Whether you're looking to troubleshoot a problem with your vehicle, share tips on car maintenance, discuss the latest automotive trends, or simply chat about your favorite cars, AutoHQ is the place for you. Our members range from seasoned gearheads to first-time car owners, each bringing their own unique perspectives to the conversation.

Why Choose AutoHQ?

  • Community-Driven: Our forum is powered by the passion of our members. With thousands of active users, you can find discussions on a wide range of topics, from the latest electric vehicles to vintage classics.
  • Expert Advice: Our community includes experienced mechanics, automotive engineers, and industry insiders who are here to offer expert advice and insights.
  • Respectful Environment: We pride ourselves on maintaining a respectful, friendly atmosphere where everyone feels welcome to participate, regardless of their level of expertise.
  • Continuous Learning: Cars and technology are constantly evolving, and so is AutoHQ. We strive to keep our community updated with the latest news, reviews, and how-to guides.

Meet the Team

Jake Thompson: With grease on his hands and a wrench in his pocket, Jake is the heart and soul of AutoHQ. A master mechanic with a love for classic muscle cars, Jake brings decades of hands-on experience to the forum.

Maria Ramirez: The voice of AutoHQ, Maria has been writing about cars for over 15 years. She’s covered everything from auto shows to industry innovations and is passionate about educating people on all things automotive.

Stefan and Todor: Stefan and Todor built the original AutoHQ platform from scratch. Their vision for a user-friendly, interactive forum has helped shape AutoHQ into the thriving community it is today.
